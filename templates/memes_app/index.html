We are working on meme emotion analysis. We call it "Memotion analysis".Here are the instructions for the annotation-
  1. Login with your Gmail id
  2. Select a meme and choose one option from each bucket ( humor, offensive, motivational, sarcastic).
  3. Mention the overall sentiment (choose the emoticons) as positive or negative .
  4. Select the basis of classification from drop down.Sometime the text is good enough to get the emotion,but sometime we need the pictorial clue as well.
    • Eg: 2. The caption provided is sufficient to sense the dislike towards a feminist man. In fact, the image has no significant role to play in the case, the provided text is good enough to sense the pun. Classification is based on caption/text only.
    • Eg: 1. the final punchline on the unavailability of Deep Learning based OCR is expressed by the man in the meme who is trying to read a small piece of paper. . Here, the facial expression of the man aids in interpreting the struggle to find OCR tutorials. If we solely process the caption, we will lose the humor and also the intended meaning. So classification is based on image+text
  5. Submit and repeat step 2.
